## 1. PROFESSIONAL BIOGRAPHY (250 words)

Dr. Nattakarn Chaihawong is a distinguished academic and aviation management specialist at the Civil Aviation Training Center (CATC), Ministry of Transport, Thailand, where she has served as a Ground Instructor in the Department of Aviation Science since 2005. Dr. Chaihawong holds a Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) in Business Management from Rajamangala University of Technology Rattanakosin, Thailand (2022), complemented by dual master's degrees from the University of California, Santa Cruz (MSc Supply Chain Management, USA) and the University of Wollongong (MSc Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Australia). This tri-continental academic formation provides her with a distinctly global perspective on aviation management systems.

Her teaching portfolio spans all academic levels — undergraduate, master's, and doctoral — encompassing airline management, air cargo operations, airport systems, research methodology, and safety management. She serves as Co-Thesis Advisor for the MM·AVM international programme, supervising students across national boundaries through advanced digital platforms.

A prolific researcher, she has authored over thirteen peer-reviewed publications in national and international journals, with scholarly focus on digital transformation in airports, passenger behaviour, airline resilience, and competency-based aviation curriculum policy. She is the lead author of two institutional textbooks published under CATC Press, including the centre's first e-textbook in aviation innovation.

Dr. Chaihawong holds certifications from ICAO, IATA, ETS, and the Airports of Thailand (AOT). Her institution's ICAO TRAINAIR PLUS Platinum status is a mark of the excellence she has helped sustain throughout her career.

## 5. RESEARCH ACHIEVEMENTS SUMMARY

**13+ Publications | SEM/CFA Methodology | Policy-Relevant Impact**

Dr. Chaihawong has built a sustained and methodologically rigorous research record over two decades. Her investigations address four core thematic clusters:

- **Digital Transformation in Aviation** — Studies on passenger acceptance of digital check-in services at Chiang Mai and Don Mueang International Airports; willingness-to-pay for digital airport services; and mobile travel agent app adoption behaviours.
